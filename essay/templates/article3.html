Algerian insurance market on the rise!

À l’occasion de la 12ᵉ édition du Salon Napec-2024, événement de référence pour l’industrie pétrolière, gazière et énergétique en Afrique du Nord, Mme Wided Belhouchet, Présidente-directrice générale de CASH Assurance, a livré ses impressions dans un entretien accordé au journal El Moudjahid.

« La réussite du Napec-2024 illustre non seulement l’intérêt croissant porté à l’Algérie comme hub énergétique, mais aussi l’importance d’une collaboration renforcée entre les acteurs publics et privés. »

The Algerian insurance market has shown remarkable resilience and steady growth in recent years. In 2024, turnover reached 181.3 billion DZD, marking a positive increase of +4.4% compared to the previous year.

At CASH Assurances, a subsidiary of Sonatrach Group, we are proud to actively contribute to this growth. Our mission goes beyond offering insurance coverage: we aim to be a reliable partner for both households and enterprises.

Looking ahead, the perspectives for the Algerian insurance market remain very promising. With digital transformation, increased awareness among the younger generation, and government policies encouraging financial inclusion, insurance is becoming an essential pillar of economic development.
